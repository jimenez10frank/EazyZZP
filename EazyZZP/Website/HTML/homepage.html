Welcome to EasyZZP

Hey there! Welcome to our ZZP Portal! We are a group of passionate developers and entrepreneurs with one goal: to make the lives of ZZP'ers (self-employed entrepreneurs) a lot easier. As a ZZP'er, you probably know how challenging it can be to keep track of everything. You run from job to job, and in the meantime, you also have to manage your administration. We know that can be quite a task. That's why we started this project. We wanted to create a place where you, as an entrepreneur, can easily manage your work, without too much hassle. A user-friendly platform that helps you stay organized and better manage your work. That way, you can spend more time on your true passion. Together, we are building this portal from the ground up, using the latest tech and ideas. We're still busy, but we're determined to create something that you and other ZZP'ers can truly use. Thank you for joining us on this adventure. Let's improve the future of self-employment together!

Features

  • Profile Management: Create and update your personal profile with details such as expertise, availability, and hourly rates.
  • Project Management: Add, track, and manage your projects with tools for deadlines, budgets, and task organization.
  • Task Tracking: Stay on top of your work with tools to create, prioritize, and mark tasks as completed.
  • Invoicing: Generate professional invoices and track payments to ensure smooth financial operations.
  • Client Management: Store and organize client details, view history, and maintain em relationships.
  • Time Tracking: Record and manage your working hours to bill clients accurately and monitor your productivity.
  • Reports and Analytics: Gain insights into your earnings, project performance, and business trends with visual reports.
  • Resource Hub: Access useful guides, templates, and resources to enhance your efficiency as a ZZP'er.
  • Communication Tools: Use built-in messaging to stay connected with clients and collaborators in one place.
  • Portfolio Display: Showcase your previous work with an attractive portfolio to attract new clients.
